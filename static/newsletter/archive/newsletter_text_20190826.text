LIBRARIES CELEBRATE BLACK HISTORY MONTH
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Drumming, storytelling and history is the focus of Richmond
    Libraries Black History Month events this October.

    Black History Month is an annual commemoration of the history,
    achievements and contributions of black people in the UK.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/09/libraries-black-history-month.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



AXEL SCHEFFLER DEBUTS THE SMEDS AND THE SMOOS - 16 NOVEMBER 2019
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    In support of Environment Trust, the world-famous illustrator Axel
    Scheffler is hosting three exclusive live events for children and
    their parents and reading his latest creation with author Julia
    Donaldson 'The Smeds and the Smoos', as well as everyone's
    favourite 'The Gruffalo'.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/09/event-scheffler-smeds-smoos.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



COUNCIL TO CONSULT ON BOROUGH'S FIRST SCHOOL STREETS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    A consultation will be launched on introducing a pilot programme
    of School Streets, following requests from concerned parents
    about road safety.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/06/lbrut-school-streets.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



BOROUGH'S SCHOOLS TOP NATIONAL RESULTS TABLES
----------------------------------------------------------------------
