RECORDALIFE - RECORD AND PRINT THE MEMOIRS OF YOUR FAMILY MEMBERS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Many people wish to write their memoirs or autobiography, or
    capture the ones of close family members, but either never get
    round to it or find it prohibitively difficult, costly and
    time-consuming. We've all heard loved ones telling amazing
    anecdotes about their lives that we'd love to capture,
    but ironically day-to-day life gets in the way of actually doing
    it.

CANCER PREVENTION JAB FOR BOYS THIS AUTUMN
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    GPs in Richmond upon Thames and the local Council are welcoming the
    introduction of the cancer preventing HPV vaccine that will be
    available to boys aged 12 to 13 in local schools from September.

    In men, many cancers of the mouth, throat, genitals and bottom are
    caused by the human papilloma virus (HPV). It can also cause warts
    on the genitals, skin, voice box and vocal cords (laryngeal
    papillomas) and verccuas.
