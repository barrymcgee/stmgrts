COVID - MEN OVER 30 - TIME TO GET HEALTH
----------------------------------------------------------------------

POP-UP CORONAVIRUS TEST CENTRE AVAILABLE THIS SEPTEMBER
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    People who think they may have contracted Coronavirus can get
    tested at a temporary pop-up testing centre in Old Deer Park,
    at selected dates this September.

       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2020/08/lbrut-pop-up-coronavirus-test-centre-available-this-september.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



ARMY ASKED TO HELP WITH TEMPORARY HAMMERSMITH BRIDGE
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    In a joint letter to the Army and Secretary of State for Defence,
    the Chair of Richmond Council's Transport and Air Quality Committee
    and local MP, have asked for military assistance evaluating and
    potentially deploying an emergency temporary bridge linking Barnes
    and Hammersmith.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2020/08/lbrut-army-asked-to-help-with-temporary-hammersmith-bridge.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



AUGUST BANK HOLIDAY WASTE AND RECYCLING COLLECTIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Following the Bank Holiday on Monday 31 August, Richmond Council
    will carry out general waste, food waste and recycling collections
    for domestic properties one day later than usual.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2020/08/lbrut-august-bank-holiday-waste-and-recycling-collections.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter
