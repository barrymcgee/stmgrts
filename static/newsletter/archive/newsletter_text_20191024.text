CAFE REOPENS AT ORLEANS HOUSE GALLERY
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    The courtyard at Orleans House Gallery, Twickenham, is once again
    home to a charming cafe serving seasonal dishes, freshly baked
    cakes and locally-roasted coffee.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archive/2019/10/orleans-house-cafe-reopens.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



NEW CHARGES FOR 'DIY' WASTE
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    All residents wanting to dispose of construction and demolition
    waste ("DIY waste") at Richmond's Household Waste and Recycling
    Centre in Kew will be charged from 2nd December.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archive/2019/10/lbrut-diy-waste-charges.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



PARKS CONSULTATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Richmond Council is asking residents to give their views on the
    Council's parks.

    The Richmond Council 'Parks Satisfaction Survey' 2019 will take
    place over the next four weeks and aims to assess overall
    satisfaction with local parks and, importantly, highlight any areas
    for improvement.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archive/2019/10/park-consultation.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



EXHIBITION 'STILLNESS' BY BOB ALDOUS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    The Artist Bob Aldous is having his third solo Exhibition at One
    Paved Court in Richmond. The show is titled "Stillness" and draw
    on the theme of the movement of water captured into a painted
    image.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archive/2019/10/exhibition-stillness-aldous.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



RICHMOND HEALTH AND CARE PLAN UPDATE
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Local people to benefit from better joined-up NHS and social care
    services, following the launch of a two-year plan to meet the
    health and care needs of people living in the borough.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archive/2019/10/richmond-health-and-care-plan.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter
