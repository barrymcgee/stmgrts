Then and Now

In 1937, with clear signs that Nazi Germany was preparing for war and mindful of the horrors inflicted by poison gas during World War 1, the British government decided to issue all 38 million civilian members of the UK population with gas masks. To this end they set up a factory in a disused mill in Blackburn, Lancashire.

Mr B and me

Although we have only met once Mr B and I have had a long business relationship, every Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday, going back decades. Our only meeting was about 15 years ago, early one summer morning. I was coming home after a night out and he was delivering milk. I strolled over and introduced myself and he did the same and that was that.
