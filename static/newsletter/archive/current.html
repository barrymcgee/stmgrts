Latest News


Richmond Council calls for government to prioritise immediate crisis in social care

Richmond Council has voiced concerns that the government's recent announcement on social care funding reforms is not enough to tackle the immediate crisis facing the sector and the thousands of local residents who rely on state support.

— read more

Calling young film makers - enter our be 'Covid-safe' competition

Young filmmakers in the borough could be in with the chance of winning great prizes, including a family paddling boarding session and tickets to Kew Gardens at Christmas, by entering a local competition to promote Covid-safety.

— read more

Stellar line-up for the 30th Richmond Literature Festival

Richmond Literature Festival has launched its 2021 programme of events for the 30th edition of the much-loved literature festival. This year's festival takes place from 5 - 21 November and will see a return to in person events following last year's move online due to the pandemic.
