Event: Take a trip down memory lane to the 1960s

Take a trip down memory lane with speaker Ian Franklin and remember what life was like growing up in the 1960s!

The talk is part of the Reminiscence Cafe, a community activity for those aged over 65 who are interested in socialising in an informal and friendly setting.

Join the new Twickenham Chamber Choir

We are a new local ladies choir based in Twickenham singing repertoire ranging from sacred and secular works to more modern pieces. If you can read music and sing in tune and are interested in applying, we would like to hear from you.
