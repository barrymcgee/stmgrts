ESSENTIAL WORKS BEGIN TO PROTECT ENDANGERED BLACK POPLARS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Richmond Council is undertaking essential conservation works
    to protect local native black poplar trees. Richmond upon Thames
    has one of the most important populations of native black poplar
    in the country.

    o  read more on the website
