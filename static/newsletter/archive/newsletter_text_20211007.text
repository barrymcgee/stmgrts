SHORT FILM 'TEARS DRY TO FROST' TO PREMIERE AT THE EXCHANGE
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Following funding from Richmond Council's Local Area Fund, local
    creatives have collaborated to produce the short film 'Tears Dry To
    Frost'. Starring Anastasia Martin, the film is about how, as she
    endures more turbulence from her partner, a young woman realises
    the need to liberate herself.

LOWER AIR POLLUTION LEVELS WITH 20MPH SCHEME
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Richmond Council has commissioned an independent assessment
    to review the levels of air pollution in Richmond upon Thames
    since the implementation of the 20mph scheme in late 2019 and early
    2020. The assessment has now shown that levels have dropped
    since the lower speed limit was introduced.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2021/10/lbrut-lower-air-pollution-levels-with-20mph-scheme.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter
