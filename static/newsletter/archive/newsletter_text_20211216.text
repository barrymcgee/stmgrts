STRUGGLING TO GET LATERAL FLOW TESTS? VISIT A LIBRARY
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    We know that many people are struggling to get lateral flow tests
    online, and want to ensure that they are regularly testing -
    particularly in the lead up to the Christmas period when people
    will be mixing more.

PCR TESTING EXPANDED IN RICHMOND
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    The mobile PCR testing facility based at Old Deer Park in Richmond
    is now seven days a week. As levels of COVID-19 remain high in the
    borough, we want to make it as easy as possible for local people
    to get tested.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2021/12/lbrut-pcr-testing-expanded-in-richmond.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter
