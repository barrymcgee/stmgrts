Latest News


Café reopens at Orleans House Gallery

The courtyard at Orleans House Gallery, Twickenham, is once again home to a charming café serving seasonal dishes, freshly baked cakes and locally-roasted coffee.

New charges for 'DIY' waste

All residents wanting to dispose of construction and demolition waste ('DIY waste') at Richmond's Household Waste and Recycling Centre in Kew will be charged from 2nd December.

— read more

Parks consultation

Richmond Council is asking residents to give their views on the Council's parks.

The Richmond Council 'Parks Satisfaction Survey' 2019 will take place over the next four weeks and aims to assess overall satisfaction with local parks and, importantly, highlight any areas for improvement.

— read more

Exhibition 'Stillness' by Bob Aldous

The Artist Bob Aldous is having his third solo Exhibition at One Paved Court in Richmond. The show is titled 'Stillness' and draw on the theme of the movement of water captured into a painted image.

— read more

Richmond Health and Care Plan update

Local people to benefit from better joined-up NHS and social care services, following the launch of a two-year plan to meet the health and care needs of people living in the borough.
