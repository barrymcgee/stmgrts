THE NIGHT A GIANT VISITED TWICKENHAM AND ST MARGARETS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    On the night of January 29th 1918, the sleeping residents
    of Twickenham and St Margarets would have been disturbed by the
    sound of powerful engines approaching from the north-east and the
    heavy 'crump' of exploding bombs. Those who got up from their beds
    to find out what was going on would have seen from their windows a
    colossal plane filling the night sky.
