COUNCIL TO REPLACE 7,000 STREET LIGHTS WITH LED LIGHTS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Over 7,000 street lights across the borough will be replaced
    with more energy-efficient LEDs from September.

    By installing more LED street lights in Richmond upon Thames, the
    Council hopes to be able to make financial savings, whilst saving
    1,000 tones of CO2 each year.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archive/2019/09/lbrut-led-streetlights.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter
