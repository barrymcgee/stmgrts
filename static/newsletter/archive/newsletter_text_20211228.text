CLOSING DOWN THE ST MARGARETS COMMUNITY WEBSITE
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    The St Margarets Community Website was started in 2005 and over the
    past 16 years I have been publishing this site regularly, but as a
    new year begins, I feel this is the right time to start closing
    down the site.
