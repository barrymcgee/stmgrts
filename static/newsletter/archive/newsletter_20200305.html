Secondary school places announced

Over 2,000 Richmond upon Thames parents applied for a secondary school place in 2020. Sixty one percent of applicants received a first preference offer and 83% received an offer at one of their first three preferences.

Are you ready for the London elections?

With the Mayor of London and London Assembly elections scheduled for Thursday 7 May, the borough's electoral services team are ready to make sure everyone who is entitled to vote is able to do so.

Refugees at Home

Refugees at Home matches generous people with spare rooms to asylum-seekers and refugees who desperately need somewhere to live, for a limited period, while they either sort out their asylum claim or their lives after getting refugee status.
