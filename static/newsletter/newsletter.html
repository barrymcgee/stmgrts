Latest News


Richmond Council backs extension of PSPO around Rosslyn Road Clinic


A Public Space Protection Order (PSPO) in place in streets around the BPAS Clinic in Rosslyn Road is to be extended for three years, following approval from all parties at Tuesday night's Full Council Meeting (30 November 2021).

Twickenham Film Studios receive planning approval


The expansion of the Oscar-winning film studio will provide jobs for local residents and attract new investment from national and international film production companies.

Footbridge over Twickenham Station to close for essential works


The public right of way footbridge from Beauchamp Road to Cole Park Road will close on Monday 6 December for one month.
